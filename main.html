Core features

Calendar

With our simplistic design, you can view your tasks, exams, and important deadlines in one centralized location. The ability to set reminders ensures that you stay on track, allowing you to prioritize tasks according to urgency.

Each task can be customized with due date, description and reminders helping you stay organized. With options to categorize tasks by subject or urgency, students can easily focus on what matters most.

As you complete assignments, you will earn coins that can be spent on various customizations for your avatar. This system not only encourages consistent effort but also fosters a sense of accomplishment.
